FAQ

What DPI is good for Etsy printables?

300 DPI is a common target for premium printable products. 240 DPI can still be acceptable depending on product expectations and print size.

Why can a file look fine on screen but fail in print?

Screen previews don't reveal print density the same way. A large print can expose low pixel density even if the image looks sharp on a phone or laptop.

Why does aspect ratio mismatch matter for listings?

If the source ratio differs from the target ratio, you'll need cropping or padding. That changes composition and can impact what the buyer receives.
